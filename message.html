Dear parents,
Your family is about to embark on a wonderful and exciting journey. Let us work hand-in-hand to create a hospitable ambience worthy of educating your child. Our Institute’s motto ‘Knowledge is Power’ sums up the ethos and commitment of all staffs to foster the potential of every child.

Success of all students is ensured by the delivery of an enriching and structural curriculum and a whole school focus on student prosperity that centers on the positive recognition of student achievements. All students are taught the skills and values necessary for success in education, life and work. They leave RIBS as calculative and well-educated citizens who bear the capability, confidence and determination to make a noteworthy contribution to our community.

Our Institute is strengthened by very supportive parents and wider community and we encourage you to become actively involved in all aspects of your child’s education. We are continually proud of our history, our values, our outstanding high standards achieved by our students.

We believe you will have a long, happy and rewarding association with Rhododendron International Boarding School(RIBS). We look forward to working with you in developing a productive and supportive partnership to nurture your child

Thank you.

D.B Gurung
Founder Principal
